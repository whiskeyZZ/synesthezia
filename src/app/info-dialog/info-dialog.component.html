Manual

Synesthezia is an audio effect playground to experiment and to create astonishing soundscapes.

First things first: Choose the sound you want to send through the effects by uploading your own sound file, by recording via microfone or by using one of the pre samples. Your uploaded and recordes sounds will be added to the "choose-sound-menu". Now get creative! Turn on the effects you want and tune them by moving the tempting black dots in the effect fields. Adding and removing of effects as well as adjusting of them can be done before playing as well as while playing. Push the play button (or the "p"-key) and enjoy. You can record the effect chain by activating the "record" button and afterwards pushing play. After you stopped playing the recorded effects will be avaiable under the "download"-menu as well as in your sound list, ready for more editing.

Effects

Delay

Horizontal: Delay-Rate from fast (left) to slow (right)
Vertical: Delay Feedback from long (bottom) to short (top)

Cutoff

Horizontal: Low-Cut from low (left) to high (right) frequencies
Vertical: High-Cut from low (bottom) to high (top) frequencies

Reverb

Horizontal: Different impact
Vertical: Dry (bottom) / Wet (top)

Crash

Horizontal: Distortion from little (left) to much (right)

Vertical: Bitcrush from much (bottom) to little (top)

Space

Horizontal: Affects the frequency range to be swapped

Vertical: De-/Increases the speed of the frequency swap
